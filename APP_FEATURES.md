# MustGym: Professional Gym Management Suite

MustGym is a high-performance, full-stack gym management application designed for elite fitness centers. It bridges the gap between administrative operations and member engagement through cutting-edge technology and a polished "Technical Dashboard" aesthetic.

---

## 1. Core Architecture & Security
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Secure partitioning for Administrators and Members.
- **Firebase Integration**: Real-time data synchronization using Firestore and secure authentication via Firebase Auth.
- **PWA Ready**: Installable on iOS and Android devices for a native-app experience.
- **SEO Optimized**: Advanced meta-tagging for every major module.

---

## 2. Interactive Member Dashboard
- **Live Gym Load**: Real-time monitoring of current gym capacity and member count.
- **Gamification Hub**: Instant visibility into earned Points, current Level, and XP progress.
- **Hydration Tracker**: Interactive "Daily 12" water logger with visual progress bars.
- **Personal Record (PR) Brief**: Quick-view of Bench Press, Squat, and Deadlift maximums.
- **Phase Tracking**: One-click access to resume the current training program.

---

## 3. Advanced Gamification & Engagement
- **Dynamic Points System**: 
  - Earn **10 Points** for daily check-ins.
  - Earn **20 Points** for completed workout sessions (verified check-out).
- **Elite Leveling**: Progressive XP system that rewards consistency and performance.
- **Badge Achievement System**: Virtual badges awarded for milestones (e.g., "Consistency King", "PR Crusher").
- **Fitness Challenges**: 
  - Community-wide events like "30-Day Squat Challenge".
  - Manual progress logging against specific targets.
  - Live competitive leaderboards for every active challenge.

---

## 4. Training & Nutrition Ecosystem
- **AI-Powered Training Generator**: Custom workout routines generated via Gemini AI based on:
  - Fitness Level (Beginner to Elite).
  - Primary Goals (Muscle Gain, Weight Loss, Strength).
  - Available Equipment (fetched directly from gym inventory).
- **AI-Powered Indian Diet Plans**: Gemini AI-driven nutritional guidance optimized for Indian palettes, featuring:
  - Macro-nutrient breakdowns (Protein, Calories).
  - Timing-specific meal suggestions.
  - Custom notes for regional preferences.
- **Progress Tracking**: Historical logging of weight and notes for every lift.

---

## 5. Terminal-Side Check-in System
- **QR Entry/Exit**: Secure scanning system for member check-in and check-out.
- **Admin Terminal Portal**: Dedicated interface for gym entrance tablets to display dynamic QR codes.
- **Attendance Verification**: Prevents duplicate entries and ensures session logging.

---

## 6. Membership Tier Management
- **Tiered Benefits**: Definition of Basic, Standard, and Elite tiers.
- **Benefit Management**: Different access levels for class bookings, personal training, and facilities.
- **Admin Assignment**: Seamless tier upgrades and status management by administrators.

---

## 7. Admin Command Center (Operations)
- **Live Alerts Console**: Real-time security and operational alerts (e.g., unauthorized scans or equipment issues).
- **Member Management**: Detailed profiles, activity logs, and status (Active/Halted/Expired) toggles.
- **Business Analytics**: 
  - Visual revenue charts (MTD, YTD).
  - Attendance heatmaps.
  - Membership distribution analysis.
- **AI Insights Engine**: Custom business suggestions and "At-Risk Member" identification powered by Gemini AI.
- **Equipment Inventory**: Maintenance tracking and reservation status for all gym assets.
- **Financial Ledger**: Recording and status tracking of membership payments in INR.
- **Global Announcements**: Centralized system to push news and alerts to all users.

---

## 8. User Experience (UX) Philosophy
- **Industrial "Must" Aesthetic**: High-contrast dark theme with neon primary accents for a professional, focused atmosphere.
- **Kinetic Feedback**: Smooth transitions and motion-powered interaction using Framer Motion.
- **Real-time Notifications**: Instant feedback for system alerts, challenge updates, and payment status.
